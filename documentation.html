Robot Project

Fundamentals of Engineering Honors

Spring Semester, 2020

Languages: C & C++

For the majority of Spring semester, 2020, I worked as part of a four-person team in my Fundamentals of Engineering Honors class (ENGR 1282.01H). Our task was to design, build, program, and document a robot to perform several tasks. Although our robot was not able to be completed as a result of online-only courses during the COVID19 pandemic, it was on track to be completed on time and in budget. The video below shows testing footage of our robot performing all of the tasks.

I was the lead programmer and documentation secondary for my team. As documentation secondary, I led my team in the development of our electronic portfolio (website) and presentation. I was responsible for the majority of the code and robot testing documentation. In addition, I helped my team stay on top of deadlines and track our progress.
