Artistic Statement

You don't see what makes something special until you look closer. I help viewers zoom in to see a different angle of objects that they may find ordinary, to experience the artistry that is found in detail. The differences within each piece that bring unique beauty to the overall image are experienced through the contrast of values and images. I want to inspire people to see the world in a new way by looking closer to what is around them.

Origamiâ€™s beauty is shown not only in the finished object, but also in the transformation of the paper. My work focuses on uniting origami objects with their crease patterns to explore the design that is the essence of each origami piece.
